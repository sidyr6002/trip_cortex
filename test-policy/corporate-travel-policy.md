# ACME Corporation — Corporate Travel & Expense Policy
**Version 3.0 | Effective January 1, 2026 | All amounts in USD**

---

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This policy governs all business travel booked on behalf of ACME Corporation. It applies to all full-time employees, contractors, and consultants traveling on company business — including domestic US travel, international travel, and travel on India routes (DEL, BOM, BLR, HYD, MAA, CCU).

---

## 2. Flight Booking Rules

### 2.1 Advance Booking Requirement

All flights must be booked at least **14 days in advance** of the travel date.

- **14+ days in advance** — No approval required, up to the applicable budget cap.
- **7–13 days in advance** — L1 Manager approval required before booking.
- **Less than 7 days in advance** — L1 Manager + Finance approval required. Written business justification must be submitted.

### 2.2 Cabin Class

Economy class is the standard for all flights.

- **US Domestic (any duration)** — Economy class only.
- **International flights under 8 hours** — Economy class only.
- **International flights 8 hours or more** — Premium Economy permitted with L1 approval. Business class requires VP-level (L3+) approval.
- **Red-eye flights (departure 22:00–05:00)** — Economy class. Seat upgrade up to $50 permitted without approval.

### 2.3 Preferred Airlines

Book with ACME's preferred carriers when available within 10% of the lowest fare:

- **US Domestic** — United Airlines, Delta Air Lines, American Airlines, Southwest Airlines
- **India routes** — Air India, IndiGo, Vistara, SpiceJet
- **International (transatlantic/transpacific)** — United Airlines, Delta Air Lines, Air India, Lufthansa, Emirates

Booking a non-preferred carrier when a preferred carrier is available at a comparable price requires L1 approval.

---

## 3. Flight Budget Caps

All caps are per one-way ticket, per person, in USD. Taxes and carrier fees are included.

| Route Category | Economy Cap | Approval if Exceeded |
|---|---|---|
| US Domestic (any route) | $400 | L1 Manager |
| US Domestic Short-Haul (under 2 hours) | $200 | L1 Manager |
| India Domestic (any route) | $150 | L1 Manager |
| India Domestic Short-Haul (under 3 hours, e.g. DEL–BOM, BOM–BLR, DEL–HYD) | $100 | L1 Manager |
| US–India (e.g. JFK–DEL, SFO–BOM, LAX–BOM) | $1,200 | L1 + Finance |
| US–Europe (e.g. JFK–LHR, ORD–CDG) | $900 | L1 + Finance |
| US–Asia Pacific (e.g. LAX–NRT, SFO–SIN) | $1,100 | L1 + Finance |
| Intra-Europe (e.g. LHR–CDG, AMS–FRA) | $350 | L1 Manager |
| Intra-Asia (e.g. SIN–BKK, HKG–NRT) | $400 | L1 Manager |

Round-trip bookings are evaluated as two separate one-way legs.

---

## 4. Employee Level Entitlements

ACME uses a 5-level classification. When an employee's level cap is lower than the route cap in Section 3, the **lower cap applies**.

| Level | Title | Domestic Cap | International Cap | Cabin Class (Intl 8h+) | Approval Authority |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| L1 | Individual Contributor | $300 | $1,000 | Economy only | L2 Manager |
| L2 | Senior / Team Lead | $400 | $1,200 | Premium Economy | L3 Director |
| L3 | Manager / Director | $500 | $1,500 | Premium Economy | L4 VP |
| L4 | VP / Senior Director | $600 | $2,000 | Business Class | L5 C-Suite |
| L5 | C-Suite / Executive | $800 | $3,000 | Business Class | Board / Self |

---

## 5. Approval Workflow

Approvals must be obtained **before** booking. Retroactive approvals are not accepted.

| Scenario | Required Approval | SLA |
|---|---|---|
| Fare within cap, 14+ days advance | No approval needed | — |
| Fare within cap, 7–13 days advance | L1 Manager | 24 hours |
| Fare within cap, less than 7 days advance | L1 + Finance | 4 hours |
| Fare exceeds cap by up to 20% | L1 Manager | 24 hours |
| Fare exceeds cap by 20–50% | L1 + Finance | 48 hours |
| Fare exceeds cap by more than 50% | L1 + Finance + L2 | 48 hours |
| Business class (L1–L3 employees) | VP (L4) + Finance | 48 hours |
| First class (any level) | C-Suite (L5) + Finance | 72 hours |

---

## 6. Reimbursement Guidelines

- Receipts required for all airfare regardless of amount.
- Reimbursement processed within 10 business days of submission.
- Frequent flyer miles earned on company travel belong to the employee.
- Seat upgrade fees are not reimbursable (except the $50 red-eye allowance in Section 2.2).
- Baggage: one checked bag reimbursable for trips of 3 or more nights. Carry-on only for shorter trips.
- Change/cancellation fees reimbursable when caused by a business requirement, with manager approval.
- No-show fees are not reimbursable.

---

## 7. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

- First class tickets (unless approved per Section 5).
- Airline club memberships (e.g. United Club, Admirals Club).
- Travel insurance purchased separately (ACME provides group coverage).
- Tickets purchased with personal miles or points.
- Fares booked after travel has already occurred.
- Personal travel extensions added to a business trip.

---

## 8. Policy Violations

Expenses submitted in violation of this policy will be denied. Repeated violations may result in loss of corporate card privileges and disciplinary action. Employees who book without required approvals are personally liable for the non-compliant portion of the fare.

---

## 9. Questions and Exceptions

Contact Finance at **travel@acme.com**. Exception requests must be submitted at least 72 hours before the booking deadline.
